What to Expect

We provide everything you need — mats, props, and a space designed for quiet reflection. Arrive 10 minutes early for your first class. Wear comfortable clothing that allows for movement. All bodies, all backgrounds, all experiences welcome.

Class Descriptions

Choose the practice that calls to you

Vinyasa Flow

60 Min • All Levels

Dynamic sequences that link breath with movement. Build strength, flexibility, and presence through flowing transitions between poses. Each class is unique, offering creative sequencing set to curated playlists.

Restorative Yoga

75 Min • Beginner

Deeply relaxing practice using bolsters, blankets, and blocks to support the body. Long-held poses (5-20 minutes) activate the parasympathetic nervous system and promote deep healing. Perfect for stress relief and recovery.

Meditation

45 Min • All Levels

Guided and silent meditation to cultivate awareness and inner stillness. Learn techniques including breath awareness, body scans, loving-kindness, and visualization. No experience necessary.

Yin Yoga

60 Min • All Levels

Slow-paced practice targeting deep connective tissues. Poses held for 3-5 minutes create space in the joints and release chronic tension patterns. Meditative and introspective.

Gentle Yoga

60 Min • Beginner

Accessible practice for those new to yoga or seeking a slower pace. Focus on fundamental poses, proper alignment, and breath awareness. Great for injury recovery or building confidence.

Breathwork

45 Min • All Levels

Explore the transformative power of conscious breathing. Learn pranayama techniques to energize, calm, and balance the nervous system. Powerful tool for managing stress and emotions.

Our Story

Still Point opened in 2018 as a response to the noise of modern life. We wanted to create a space where people could step away from constant stimulation and reconnect with themselves.

Our studio is intentionally minimal — natural light, warm wood, soft textiles. No mirrors, no distractions. Just you and your practice. We believe yoga and meditation are not escapes from reality, but practices that help you meet life with greater clarity and resilience.

Our teachers come from diverse lineages and backgrounds, united by a commitment to authentic, trauma-informed teaching. We welcome all bodies, all identities, all levels of experience. Whether you're seeking physical strength, mental calm, or spiritual connection, there's a place for you here.

Meet Our Teachers

Sarah Chen

Lead Instructor

500-hour certified teacher with 12 years of experience. Trained in Vinyasa, Yin, and trauma-sensitive yoga. Sarah's classes blend precise alignment with poetic cues and carefully curated playlists.

Maya Patel

Meditation & Restorative

Certified meditation teacher and Reiki practitioner. Maya studied in India and brings ancient wisdom to contemporary practice. Her classes are havens of deep rest and self-compassion.

James Wright

Breathwork & Yin

Former athlete who found yoga after injury. James specializes in functional movement and pranayama. His classes are grounding, accessible, and surprisingly challenging.
