From the Islands to Your Street

Island Flame started in my backyard — weekend cookouts where family recipes met open fire. What began as jerk chicken for friends turned into lines around the block.

I grew up in Kingston, learning to cook from my grandmother. She taught me that good Caribbean food isn't just about spice — it's about patience, respect for ingredients, and cooking with soul.

Now we bring those same flavors to the streets, using her original jerk marinade and traditional techniques. Every plate we serve carries the islands with it — bold, authentic, and made with love.
