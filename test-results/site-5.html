A Real Nashville Honky-Tonk

The Rusty Spur has been pouring cold beer and hosting live country music since the day we opened our doors. No fancy cocktails, no cover bands playing pop hits — just the kind of dive bar where real musicians play and real people drink.

Our stage has seen everyone from road-worn session players to up-and-comers trying to make it in Music City. The walls are covered in signed guitars, the floor's got decades of scuff marks, and the bar's sticky in all the right places.
