About Us

Our Story

Where Luxury Meets Love

Founded with a passion for exceptional pet care, Paws and Prose brings together certified master groomers and a dedication to creating a stress-free, spa-like experience for your beloved companions.

Our Art Deco-inspired salon combines vintage elegance with modern techniques, ensuring every pet receives personalized attention in a calming, beautiful environment. We believe grooming is both an art and a science—each cut is tailored to enhance your pet's natural beauty while prioritizing their comfort and wellbeing.

From our organic, cruelty-free products to our gentle handling techniques, every detail is designed to make your pet feel cherished. Because they deserve nothing less than the royal treatment.
