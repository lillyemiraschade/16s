We're Rethinking
Plant-Based

Sprout started in a tiny Brooklyn kitchen in 2019 when our founder, a professionally trained chef, got tired of bland vegan takeout. We knew plants could do better — way better.

Built by Chefs,
Not Algorithms

Every dish we make is designed by real chefs with decades of culinary training. We don't optimize for scale — we optimize for flavor.

Our kitchen team comes from Michelin kitchens, neighborhood bistros, and family tables around the world. We treat vegetables like the stars they are.

We cook in small batches, taste everything ourselves, and never cut corners. If we wouldn't serve it to our own family, it doesn't go in your box.

Our Commitments

Organic First

Over 90% of our produce is certified organic. When it's not, it's from a farm we know personally.

Fair Wages

Everyone on our team — from chefs to drivers — makes at least $20/hour with full benefits.

Local Impact

We donate surplus meals to community fridges and partner with urban farms in our delivery zones.
