Our Philosophy

"Financial planning isn't about predicting the future—it's about preparing for it."

We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and honest communication. Our fiduciary duty means we always act in your best interest, never recommending products or strategies that don't align with your goals.

Services

Comprehensive wealth management tailored to your unique situation, goals, and values.

Retirement Planning
We help you answer the critical questions: When can I retire? How much do I need? What's the best withdrawal strategy? Our retirement planning process covers income projections, Social Security optimization, Medicare planning, and tax-efficient withdrawal strategies to help you retire with confidence and maintain your lifestyle throughout retirement.
Investment Management
Our investment philosophy emphasizes diversification, cost efficiency, and discipline. We construct portfolios aligned with your risk tolerance and time horizon, using low-cost index funds and strategic asset allocation. Regular rebalancing and ongoing monitoring ensure your portfolio stays on track as markets shift and your needs evolve.
Tax Planning
Taxes are often one of the largest expenses you'll face. We work proactively to minimize your tax burden through strategic planning around retirement account withdrawals, Roth conversions, tax-loss harvesting, charitable giving strategies, and coordination with your CPA to ensure every aspect of your financial life is tax-efficient.
Estate Planning
Estate planning ensures your assets transfer according to your wishes while minimizing taxes and complications. We coordinate with your attorney to review beneficiary designations, trust structures, gifting strategies, and legacy goals. Our focus is on creating a plan that protects your family and reflects your values.
Insurance Analysis
Insurance is a critical component of financial planning, protecting your family and assets from unexpected events. We conduct comprehensive reviews of your life, disability, and long-term care insurance to ensure you have appropriate coverage at competitive rates, with regular reviews as your needs change.
College Planning
Funding education is one of the largest expenses families face. We help you evaluate savings strategies, compare 529 plans, understand financial aid implications, and balance education funding with other financial goals to ensure you're prepared without sacrificing your retirement security.

About Summit Wealth

Independent financial planning built on trust, transparency, and a fiduciary commitment to always act in your best interest.

Our Story

Summit Wealth was founded on a simple belief: financial planning should be transparent, conflict-free, and always focused on the client's best interest. After years of working in larger firms where product sales often took priority over planning, we decided to build a different kind of practice.

As an independent, fee-only firm, we have no ties to banks, insurance companies, or investment product manufacturers. This means our recommendations are based solely on what's best for you—not what earns us the highest commission.

Our Approach

We believe in building long-term relationships. Most of our clients have been with us for years, and many have referred their family members and friends. This loyalty comes from our commitment to honest communication, proactive planning, and always being available when you need us.

Financial planning isn't a one-time event—it's an ongoing relationship. Markets change, life circumstances evolve, and tax laws shift. We're here to help you navigate all of it with confidence and clarity.

Fiduciary Commitment

As a fiduciary, we are legally and ethically bound to act in your best interest at all times. This means:

  • We never accept commissions or payments from investment products
  • Our recommendations are based solely on your needs, not our compensation
  • We disclose all fees and potential conflicts of interest upfront
  • We prioritize your financial goals over our business growth
