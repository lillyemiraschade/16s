Brooklyn Born, Barber Bred

We opened Fade and Fortune in 2015 with a simple mission: bring back the art of the classic barbershop experience. No rushed cuts, no assembly line feel — just skilled barbers who take pride in their craft.

Our team trained in old-school technique and modern trends, so whether you want a clean fade or a vintage pompadour, we've got you covered. We use premium products, hot towels, and straight razors because that's how it's supposed to be done.
